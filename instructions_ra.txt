The samplelist.txt is what you give it as the $SAMPLELIST argument, it's supposed to be a list of base sample names. The script then assumes that your filtered files are named ${SAMPLE}_1.filtered.fq.gz/${SAMPLE}_2.filtered.fq.gz 

So, for example, if my reads are called LK_Cm1_1.filtered.fq.gz and LK_Cm1_2.filtered.fq.gz, then the sample name on the sample list should be LK_Cm1

If your reads are named differently (like LK_Cm1.R1.fq.gz) then you need to change the path accordingly in the script, before it passes it to the job
